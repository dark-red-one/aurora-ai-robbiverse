Frequently Asked Questions

What is TestPilot?
A platform that helps CPG brands test product performance before launch using realistic eCommerce simulations and real shopper behavior. Results in 72 hours—faster and cheaper than traditional test markets.
How fast and cost-effective is it?
72 hours for results. 10x faster and 5x cheaper than traditional test markets. Starting at $49 per shopper with tests from 50 shoppers minimum (~$2,450 per test).

TestPilot recruits from over 1.2 million U.S.-based shoppers using demographic filters, psychographic groups, or custom screening questions.
