What makes Robbie different from other AI tools?
Most AI tools are trained on generic data. Robbie was trained specifically for CPG by experts with decades of experience at P&G, Walmart, Amazon, and Nestlé, plus a PhD statistician. This specialized training is how we deliver both quality insights AND 72-hour speed.
How does TestPilot capture real shopper behavior?
We create realistic digital shelf simulations (Amazon, Walmart) with your product competing against 11 real competitors. Real shoppers from our 1.2M+ panel browse, compare, click, and make purchase decisions just like they would on actual retail sites.
Why 72 hours instead of weeks?
Traditional research takes weeks because human analysts need time to review data, identify patterns, and write reports. Robbie does this analysis in hours - examining every click, scroll, and purchase across hundreds of shoppers simultaneously.
